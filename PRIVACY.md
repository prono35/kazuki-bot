# Privacy Policy for Kazuki Bot

**Last Updated: January 11, 2026**

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Kazuki Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information:
- **User IDs**: Discord user IDs for memory and personalization
- **Server IDs**: Server/guild IDs where the bot is active
- **Channel IDs**: Channel IDs where bot is enabled
- **Message Content**: Only messages directly sent to the bot for AI responses
- **Usernames**: For personalizing responses
- **Server Information**: Server name, member count (public information)

### We DO NOT Collect:
- ❌ Passwords or authentication tokens
- ❌ Personal information (email, phone, address)
- ❌ Messages not directed to the bot
- ❌ Voice chat data
- ❌ Payment information
- ❌ IP addresses

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- ✅ Provide AI chat responses
- ✅ Remember conversation context (chat memory)
- ✅ Improve bot functionality
- ✅ Enable/disable bot in specific channels
- ✅ Generate personalized responses

## Data Storage

- **Chat Memory**: Stored temporarily for conversation context (can be cleared with `/reset`)
- **Channel Settings**: Stored to remember which channels have bot enabled
- **Retention**: Chat memory is stored until user requests deletion
- **Security**: Data is stored securely and not shared with third parties

## Data Sharing

We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your data
- ❌ Share data with advertisers
- ❌ Give data to third parties
- ❌ Use data for marketing

We MAY share data only:
- ✅ When required by law
- ✅ To protect against abuse
- ✅ With your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- ✅ **Access**: Request what data we have about you
- ✅ **Delete**: Clear your chat memory using `/reset`
- ✅ **Opt-out**: Remove bot from your server anytime
- ✅ **Control**: Admins can disable bot in channels with `/toggleai`

## Data Deletion

To delete your data:
1. Use `/reset` command to clear chat memory
2. Remove bot from your server
3. Contact us at: [support server](https://discord.gg/9T77GN8dm3)

## Third-Party Services

We use:
- **NVIDIA AI API**: For AI responses (subject to their privacy policy)
- **Discord API**: For bot functionality (subject to Discord's privacy policy)

## Children's Privacy

The bot is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
